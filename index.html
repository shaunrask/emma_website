About Me

About Me

Hello! My name is Emma Rask (she/her), and I'm a singer, actor, and musician from Upstate New York. I am a senior at the State University of New York at Fredonia, where I am earning my BFA in Musical Theatre with a minor in Dance.

My passions include animals (ask me about my pets), my family, piano, karaoke, and tap dancing! A fun fact about me is that when I’m bored, I like to solve Algebra problems!

I found my love for the arts very early in life. In elementary school, my parents took me to countless open mic events so I could play the piano and sing! And after my first musical, I knew I never wanted to leave the stage.

I just had the opportunity to perform my BFA Senior Recital (a few short clips are linked in the Reels section of this website)! And for this year’s Mainstage season, I played Chutney in Legally Blonde and I am starting the rehearsal process for the One Act Play Festival in a few weeks.
